## Learn

Qualitification is about: value, stakeholders and process. It is not BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timing). There are three levels of qualification: Discovery, Delivery & Consensus.

MEDDPPICC stands for:

- Metrics

- Decision Process
- Paper Process
- Implicate Pain
