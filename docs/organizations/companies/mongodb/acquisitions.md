# MongoDB Acquisitions

MongoDB has acquired 4 companies since 2014.

## WireTiger (2014)

> An innovator in database storage engine technology.
> WiredTiger will contribute to the performance, scalability, and hardware efficiency of MongoDB for all users with the upcoming release, MongoDB 3.0.

🌐 [Press release](https://www.mongodb.com/company/newsroom/press-releases/wired-tiger) - Dec 16, 2014

## mLab (2018)

> A San Francisco-based cloud database service.
> MongoDB's acquisition of mLab will help deepen its relationships with developer-centric startup communities, a segment with which mLab has been very successful.
> mLab currently has approximately one million hosted databases on its platform across both free and paid tiers.

🌐 [Press release](https://www.mongodb.com/company/newsroom/press-releases/mongodb-strengthens-global-cloud-database-with-acquisition-of-mlab) - Oct 9, 2018

## Realm (2019)

> The company behind the Realm mobile database and synchronization platform.

🌐 [Press release](https://www.mongodb.com/company/newsroom/press-releases/mongodb-strengthens-mobile-offerings-with-acquisition-of-realm) - Apr 24, 2019

## Voyage AI (2025)

> A pioneer in state-of-the-art embedding and reranking models that power next-generation AI applications.
> Integrating Voyage AI's technology with MongoDB will enable organizations to easily build trustworthy, AI-powered applications by offering highly accurate and relevant information retrieval deeply integrated with operational data.

🌐 [Press release](https://www.mongodb.com/press/mongodb-announces-acquisition-of-voyage-ai) - Feb 24, 2025
