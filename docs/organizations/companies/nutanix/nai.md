# Nutanix Enterprise AI

> The Nutanix Enterprise AI (NAI) solution offers endpoint APIs for the leading LLM providers, including NVIDIA NIM and Hugging Face, making it easy for organizations to deploy a wide range of GenAI models securely, on-premises or in the public cloud.
> NAI includes a simple UI, role-based access controls (RBAC), and the capability for untethered operations (dark site or air-gapped deployments) to simplify the operation, monitoring, and adaptation of AI models (LLMs) with enterprise resiliency, Day 2 operations, and compliance
