## Culture

### Values

GitLab’s six core values CREDIT are:

* 🤝 Collaboration
* 📈 Results for Customers
* ⏱️ Efficiency
* 🌐 Diversity, Inclusion & Belonging
* 👣 Iteration
* 👁️ Transparency
